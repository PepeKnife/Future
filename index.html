The future of robots

Robots will replace us

Robots will replace humans in a lot of things, because they are better in repetitive tasks and in tasks that require precision and speed. As automation technology continues to improve, it is likely that more jobs in these areas will be taken over by robots. But there are some skills that robots can’t do, such as tasks that require emotional intelligence or social skills.

Space research

Space research already use robots to complement the work of humans. Robots are taking part in space exploration, in missions that are dangerous for humans. For example, robots have been used to explore Mars and other planets, collecting samples and data, and carry repairs and maintenance on satellites.
